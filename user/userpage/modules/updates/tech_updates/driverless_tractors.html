DRIVERLESS TRACTORS

What are they?

As the name suggests, driverless tractors are farm vehicles that operate without the presence of a human inside the tractor itself. They are programmed to independently observe their position, decide speed, and avoid obstacles such as people, animals, or objects in the field while performing their task. They operate simply with the aid of a supervisor monitoring the progress remotely using an electronic device such as tablet.

How they help?

  • They promote fatigue-free farming since the farmer does not have to spend hours tilling the land.
  • They also support all weather farming, be it summer or winter or rainy since the farmer can control the vehicle while at a safe-space. It eliminates any health hazards that can be caused while handling the chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
  • They increase the efficiency of farming and hence give a better yield.

Cost?

The cost depends on variants. However, theaverage cost of a driverless tractor in India is around Rs. 8,00,000 (It's only an expected price. Contact your tractor dealer for the exact estimate).
