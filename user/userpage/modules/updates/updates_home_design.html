Buzzing facts

1. About 60% to 70% of India's population depend upon agriculture for their livelihood. The nation salutes you!

2. India has the 2nd largest agricultural output in the world, and as of 2013, it is the 7th largest exporter of agricultural goods.

3. Statistics show that the net foreign earnings from agricultureare at par with the IT sector, as of 2013.

4. States which make a substantial contribution to the agricultural industry of the country are West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh and Punjab.

5. Uttar Pradesh is the largest agricultural contributor of India. It has a cultivation area of 16.68 million hectares (around 76% of its total land area).
